# Repo-wide Grade S and E5 promotion

Grade S requires a complete Grade A workstream bundle plus an independently
signed continuous-verification report. The verifier organization must be
separate from operations and continuously observe every critical control across
at least two regions and two providers. Its report binds the same artifact and
the exact set of Grade A evidence heads.

The candidate must score at least 90, keep every critical control at 80 or
higher, remain L4, provide E5 evidence, pass the release gate, and have zero
residual critical gaps. Internal CI, periodic red-team exercises, stale reports,
single-provider monitoring, or relabeled Grade A receipts fail closed.

The implementation is a promotion verifier, not E5 evidence. No current repo is
promoted until a real independent continuous verifier supplies the receipt.
